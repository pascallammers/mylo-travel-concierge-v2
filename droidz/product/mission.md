## Product Vision

MYLO is an AI-powered travel concierge that transforms how travelers discover, plan, and optimize their journeys. By combining advanced AI capabilities with deep travel industry expertise, MYLO helps users unlock premium travel experiences through intelligent flight search, award booking optimization, and personalized recommendations.

---

## Problem Statement

### Pain Points We Solve

1. **Complex Award Booking**: Finding award availability across multiple loyalty programs is time-consuming and requires expertise
2. **Information Overload**: Travelers spend hours comparing flights, prices, and loyalty program options
3. **Missed Opportunities**: Users often pay cash for flights when award seats are available
4. **Fragmented Research**: Travel research requires multiple tools, tabs, and sources
5. **Premium Access Barriers**: Business and First Class travel seems inaccessible to everyday travelers

### The Gap in the Market

Traditional flight search engines focus on paid fares. Loyalty program websites are siloed and difficult to navigate. There's no unified, intelligent assistant that helps travelers maximize their points and miles while providing comprehensive travel intelligence.

---

## Solution Overview

MYLO is a conversational AI travel concierge that:

- **Searches flight availability** across cash fares and award programs
- **Provides personalized recommendations** based on user preferences and history
- **Remembers context** through conversation memory for continuous assistance
- **Researches deeply** using web, academic, and specialized travel sources
- **Connects to user data** via Google Drive and other integrations (Pro feature)

---

## Target Users

### Primary Persona: The Points & Miles Enthusiast
- **Demographics**: 25-55 years old, frequent travelers, tech-savvy
- **Behaviors**: Actively collects points/miles, follows travel blogs, optimizes credit card spend
- **Goals**: Fly business/first class using points, maximize redemption value
- **Pain Points**: Tracking award availability, complex booking rules, time-consuming searches

### Secondary Persona: The Premium Traveler
- **Demographics**: 30-60 years old, business professionals, higher income
- **Behaviors**: Values comfort over price, travels for business and leisure
- **Goals**: Seamless travel planning, premium cabin experiences
- **Pain Points**: Time constraints, comparing options, finding best value

### Tertiary Persona: The Curious Explorer
- **Demographics**: 22-40 years old, adventure seekers, budget-conscious
- **Behaviors**: Uses technology to plan trips, researches extensively
- **Goals**: Discover new destinations, find deals, learn about travel hacking
- **Pain Points**: Overwhelming options, learning curve for points/miles
